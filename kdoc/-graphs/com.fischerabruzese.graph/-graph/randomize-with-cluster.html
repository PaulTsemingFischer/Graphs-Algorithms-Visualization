randomizeWithCluster

fun randomizeWithCluster(numClusters: Int, minEdgeWeight: Int, maxEdgeWeight: Int, intraClusterConnectedness: Double, interClusterConnectedness: Double, random: Random = Random)

Randomizes multiple graphs with p = intraClusterConnectedness; Then combines the graph and connects the possible edges between them with p = interClusterConnectedness.

inter/intra is essentially the clusteriness of the graph.

Parameters

numClusters

The number of clusters to split the graph into.

maxEdgeWeight

The maximum weight of a connection(exclusive).

intraClusterConnectedness

The probability of a connection being made within a cluster. Must be between 0 and 1.

interClusterConnectedness

The probability of a connection being made between clusters. Must be between 0 and 1.

random

A random object that determines what graph is constructed
